Food Recipe Lists are:

Certainly! Here's an extended list of popular Indian dishes:

1. Ananas menaskai:pineapple cooked in Jaggery and tamarind gravy.

2. Attu: Several types are made with several type of flours including rice flour, urad dal,semolina and wheat flour. This is the name of it in Andhra Pradesh which is also called Dosa. Attu is coarse than Dosa..

3. Aval kesari: Paneer (cottage cheese) in a rich and buttery tomato gravyroasted flat rice flour cooked with sugar and dry fruits.

4. Avial:Coconut paste, curd mixed with vegetables and some spices.

5. Baida roti: fried minced chicken stuffed in Egg roll.

6. Halwa:Maida or Sugarcane fritters which are known as Halwa in South India.

7.Bhajji: Vegetable or onion fritters which are known as Pakodas in North India and Pakistani cuisine.

8. Biryani: Spicy rice dish with vegetables or chicken or mutton or fish or prawns.

9.Bisi bele bath (Karnataka):Rice preparation with vegetables.

10. Bonda: Snack. Potatoes, gram flour.

11. Chettinadu Chicken: Dish made chicken and spices.

12. Chicken 65: Popular deep fried chicken preparation. Chicken, onion, ginger.

13. Currivepillai sadam (Tamil Nadu):Curry leaves and rice.

14. Dibba rotti: It is a breakfast item made in Andhra pradesh and is also called Minaparotti. Made with rice, urad dal. Tastes good with a chutney.

15. Dosa: Pancake/Hopper. Ground rice, urad dal.

16. Double ka meetha: Bread crumbs fried in ghee and dipped in milk and sugar syrup.

17. Ennai Kathirikkai: Ennai Kathirikkai.

18. Goli bajje: a snack with Gram flour.

19. Hyderabadi biryani: Biryani cooked in hyderabadi style.

20. Idiyappam: Steamed rice noodles or vermicelli with Ground rice.

21. Idli: Steamed cake of fermented rice and pulse flour. Rice, urad dal.

22. Indian omelette: Egg omelette or veg omelette.

23. Kaara kozhambu (Tamil Nadu): a dish used with rice made of chilli powder and tamarind.

24. Kanji:a rice porridge.

25. Keerai koottu (Tamil Nadu): Green leaves kootu.

26. Keerai masiyal: Ground green leaves used as a side dish for rice or mixed with rice.

27. Kerala Beef Fry: Beef, onions, spices, coconut, curry leaves.

28. Kodubale: mixture rice flour, soji, shredded coconut, red chillies, cumin*, salt in the shape of a ring fried in oil.

29. Koottu: Vegetable, daal or lentil mixture boiled in water.

30. Nandu omelette: an omelette with pieces of crab and spices.

31. Puttu: Ground rice, jaggery, cardamom powder, mixed and steam cooked.

32. Rasam:A spicy and sour soup usually made with tamarind, tomatoes, pepper and other south Indian spices. Usually eaten with rice. .

33. Sambar: Lentil soup cooked with vegetables and a blend of south Indian spices (masala). Usually taken with rice, idli, dosa, pongal or upma..

34. Sevai: Kind of rice vermicelli mixed with either tamarind or lemon or coconut.

35. Thattai:Type of puri made with rice, gram , urad dal flour.

This list provides a glimpse into the vast and diverse world of Indian cuisine, showcasing the regional variations and culinary creativity that make it truly exceptional.
